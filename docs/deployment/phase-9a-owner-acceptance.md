# Phase 9A owner acceptance

This checklist remains pending until hosted deployment and a signed-in owner session are available.

## Player economy

- Record a player’s DUST, open history, and confirm friendly starter/activity/purchase labels.
- Open Village Supply Shop; verify only ordinary published items and prices appear.
- Buy once, refresh, and verify one charge, one item grant, one persistent receipt.
- Replay the request; verify no second charge or item.
- Verify insufficient DUST, full inventory, limit, cooldown, stale price, maintenance, suspension,
  and disabled-shop states are friendly and lossless.
- Confirm protected/starter/activity-temporary items are absent and gameplay input pauses while the
  shop/history panel is open.
- Check keyboard focus, Escape, focus restoration, touch targets, polite announcements, contrast,
  reduced motion, UI scale 90–120%, and the specified phone/tablet/desktop viewport matrix.

## Operations

- Run single-player and bounded global reconciliation; confirm balanced output and no auto-rewrite.
- Review a local mismatch fixture and safe risk signal.
- Create/approve a low correction; retry approval; confirm one immutable ledger receipt.
- Verify a high correction needs two distinct reviewers and a creator cannot self-approve.
- Walk a policy and shop draft through validation, review submission, independent approval, future
  scheduling, and separately confirmed publication; confirm no step activates early.
- Reactivate a prior published policy and shop with the controlled rollback action; confirm the
  historical rows remain immutable and only the authoritative active pointer changes.
- Confirm Read-only Analyst cannot mutate, Customer Support cannot approve, Content Manager cannot
  publish, and no Set Balance control exists.

## Simulation and `$STAR`

- Repeat a 100-player/30-day simulation with the same seed and compare identical output.
- Run 1,000/90 and 10,000/180 planning scenarios; confirm no real balance, ledger, inventory, or
  cooldown changes.
- Review Candidates A–D and the conservative Candidate D recommendation; confirm it remains
  unpublished unless the owner separately chooses and explicitly publishes a reviewed version.
- Confirm the existing STAR access threshold remains 1,000 unless separately owner-configured, the
  mint remains environment-driven, and no approval/transfer/claim is requested.
