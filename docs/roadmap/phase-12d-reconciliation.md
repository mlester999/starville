## Reconciliation checks

A bounded reconciliation pass should report:

- any V1 byte drift or missing V1 manifest path;
- any V2 source/runtime/thumbnail missing from its manifest or report;
- orphan V2 media or unsafe/case-mismatched path;
- manifest, coverage, size, or checksum drift;
- stable-key, alias, fallback, variant, anchor, footprint, collision, or rotation divergence between
  V1 and V2;
- a V2 delivery missing `bundled_candidate` or exact manifest `2.0.0`;
- an uploaded delivery claiming a bundled identity;
- a published unpinned context selecting V2;
- a terrain key selected by the renderer but absent from the server-derived dependency set;
- Composer, Draft Preview, Game Test, and production-renderer identity mismatch;
- a repository-authored entry claiming final without owner identity, timestamp, and evidence;
- any candidate activation, published-pin rewrite, upload-history deletion, or world publication;
- any runtime/thumbnail/frame or total pack budget breach;
- any character state/direction mapping missing from the 24-cell matrix; and
- any user-owned art that a deterministic generator would overwrite.

Safe automated work is limited to local inspection, deterministic repository-output generation,
tests, and recommendations. Reconciliation must not activate a version, promote a quality label,
record owner acceptance, delete history, rewrite a pin, publish a world, mutate player/economy
state, push a migration, deploy, commit, or push Git.
